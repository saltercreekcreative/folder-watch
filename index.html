A real ingest pipeline, sitting in a panel.

live

Questions

Good to know.

Does it work while the panel is closed?

It syncs whenever Premiere is open and watching — and it resumes the same watch automatically every time you relaunch.

Will it duplicate my media?

No. It recognises clips already in your project and adopts them instead of importing a second copy.

Is it safe for a live project?

Yes — it's non-destructive. It only adds, refreshes, and takes missing media offline. It never deletes clips or bins.

Mac?

Windows for now (Premiere Pro 2020 / 14.0 and up). Mac support is on the roadmap.
