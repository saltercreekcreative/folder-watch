Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

This policy explains what data is — and isn't — involved when you use the Folder Watch website and software. Folder Watch is made by Salter Creek PTE. LTD. ("Salter Creek", "we", "us"), based in Singapore. Contact: saltercreektools@gmail.com.

The short version: The Folder Watch software runs entirely on your own computer and does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. The only personal data we ever handle is what you give us directly — your order details (through Gumroad) and any emails you send us.

The software

Folder Watch runs locally inside Adobe Premiere Pro on your machine. It watches folders you choose, imports media into your project, and stores its own settings and sync state in a local folder on your computer (for example, under %APPDATA% on Windows). It contains no analytics, no tracking, and no telemetry, and it does not send any information about you, your projects, or your files to us or to anyone else. It does not require an account or an internet connection to run.

Buying Folder Watch

Purchases are handled by Gumroad, our payment processor and merchant of record. When you buy, Gumroad collects the information needed to process the order — typically your name, email address, and payment details — under Gumroad's own privacy policy. We do not see or store your full payment-card details. We do receive limited order information from Gumroad (such as your email address and which product you bought) so we can deliver the Software and provide support.

This website

This site is a static page hosted on GitHub Pages. Like most web hosts, GitHub may automatically log standard technical information (such as your IP address and browser type) for security and to operate the service. The site also loads two third-party resources:

  • Google Fonts — for typography. Google may receive your IP address in order to serve the fonts.
  • Gumroad checkout overlay — when you click a buy button, Gumroad's checkout loads so you can pay without leaving the page. Gumroad may set cookies and process data as part of checkout, under its own policy.

We do not set our own cookies and we do not run advertising or analytics trackers on this site.

Email & support

If you email us (for support, a refund request, or anything else), we receive your email address and whatever you choose to include in your message. We use it only to respond to and help you, and to keep a record of support requests.

How we use and share data

We use the limited data described above only to deliver the Software, process refunds, and provide support. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not share it for advertising. The only third parties involved are the service providers that make the product work: Gumroad (payments and delivery), GitHub (website hosting), and Google Fonts (typography).

Your rights

You can ask us what personal data we hold about you (essentially your order record and any support emails), and ask us to correct or delete it, by emailing saltercreektools@gmail.com. For data held by Gumroad as part of your purchase, you can also contact Gumroad directly. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or your local data-protection law.

Children

Folder Watch is a professional tool and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always posted on this page, with the "last updated" date shown above.
